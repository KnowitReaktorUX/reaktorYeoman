About reaktor:gulp

reactor:gulp is a "Yeoman Generator". Since you're seeing this page, you've used it and answered "yes" to using Fabricator Assemble. You can read more information about Fabricator Assemble here: https://github.com/fbrctr/fabricator-assemble. However, to get a basic understanding, just have a look at the source-code! Happy development!
